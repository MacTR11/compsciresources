> 🖨️ **Use the PDFs** in [`printable-pdfs/subtopic-quizzes/`](../printable-pdfs/subtopic-quizzes/) — print the question pages and hand them out; the answer key is at the end of each (print it for yourself, or let students self-mark). The `.md` files here are the editable source.

## How to use
- **Starter / plenary:** Section A (multiple choice) makes a quick 5-minute retrieval check.
- **Homework:** print Sections A–C, keep the answer key.
- **Self-test:** do it closed-book, then mark with the key and RAG-rate on your [tracker](../guides/self-assessment-tracker.md).
- **Intervention:** target a student's weak subtopics precisely.

Each quiz reconciles exactly to its stated total. To regenerate the PDFs after editing a quiz, run `python3 revision-tools/build-pdfs.py`.
